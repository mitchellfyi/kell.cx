# Kell.cx Outreach

## Target Customer Profile

**Primary:** Startup founders in competitive markets
- Developer tools / AI coding assistants
- SaaS companies with 3+ direct competitors
- Series A/B companies with resources but no CI team

**Secondary:** Product managers, VCs tracking portfolio/market

## Value Proposition

> "Know what your competitors ship before your customers tell you."

Daily digest of:
- Changelog/product updates
- Pricing changes
- Job postings (signals hiring intent)
- Social mentions (coming soon)

**Price point:** $49/mo per competitor tracked (starting point)
