Questions

What's the catch?
No catch. We don't have dashboards, CRM integrations, or sales battlecards. We just send you a daily email with what changed. That's the whole product.
How is this so cheap compared to Crayon/Klue?
Those tools are built for enterprise sales teams. They have account managers, onboarding calls, and features most people never use. Kell is self-serve and focused on one thing: actionable daily intel.
What competitors can I track?
Any company with a public website. We monitor their website, changelog, careers page, pricing, and mentions across Hacker News, Reddit, Product Hunt, and tech news sites.
Can I change my competitors later?
Yes. Email us anytime and we'll update your list within 24 hours. (Self-serve dashboard coming soon.)
